ABOUT

Forever for a while tells the story of Vansh, a reserved and introverted boy, whose world is brightened by a girl named Yuoko. She is vibrant, joyfull and adds color to everything she touches.


Despite their contrasting personalities, they form a happy couple. However, their relationship faces a major challenge when Yuoko leaves the city to persue her dreams.


The story unfolds as both of them navigate the unexpected changes that follow, testing their love and resilience.
